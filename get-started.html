Get Started with OpenWrap

Follow these steps and you'll be wraped up in minutes!

  1. Download and extract the OpenWrap console
  2. Run o.exe
  3. When prompted, type i to install OpenWrap into your path.

OpenWrap is now installed and ready to do your bidding.
